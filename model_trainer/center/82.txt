Barnsley bring in defender Watson on one-year deal Image source, Rex Features Image caption, Tennai Watson made 50 League One appearances in two seasons with Charlton Athletic Published 8 August 2025 Barnsley have signed former Charlton Athletic right-back Tennai Watson on a one-year deal. The 28-year-old helped the Addicks win promotion to the Championship last season before being released in the summer. "Tennai is a player who knows what it takes to be successful in this division," boss Conor Hourihane told the club website., external "He's athletic, disciplined and brings a competitive edge that will benefit the whole group."
