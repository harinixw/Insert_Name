Last year, the calendars were posted by the end of July. Josh Blackman | 8.6.2025 1:05 AM As of August 5, 2025, the Supreme Court has not yet posted the argument calendars for the October and November sittings. For OT 2024, the October and November calendars were posted on July 26, 2024. For OT 2023, the October calendar was posted on July 14, 2023. The Court is behind schedule.
