The Story Behind a Famous Photo of a Starving Child in Gaza
Photo of starving child in Gaza goes viral and ignites debate : State of the World from NPR A photo of an emaciated child in Gaza has gone viral, with many in Israel claiming it depicts false information. Our producer in Gaza meets the child and his family, we hear about how he is doing now and about the food insecurity many in Gaza are facing.
