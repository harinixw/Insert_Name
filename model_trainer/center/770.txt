Boy, 4, dies after being hit by bus as he walked out of hospital
Boy, 4, dies after being hit by bus as he walked out of hospital in Margate, Kent Zaahir Jan was struck after leaving the Queen Elizabeth, Queen Mother Hospital in Margate, where he was visiting his grandmother. Friday 8 August 2025 10:25, UK Image: Zaahir Jan. Pic: Azaria Green/Gofundme Why you can trust Sky News A four-year-old boy has died after being struck by a bus outside a hospital in Kent. Zaahir Jan had been visiting his grandmother at the Queen Elizabeth, Queen Mother Hospital in Margate when the incident happened just after 4pm on Thursday. Kent Police said the bus, a white single-decker vehicle, was travelling near the entrance to the A&E department when Zaahir left the hospital on foot and was struck. He was taken inside the hospital but died shortly after. Image: Pic: Azaria Green/Gofundme His mother, Azaria Green, described her son as a "sweet, funny boy with a cheeky smile", on a GoFundMe page. "Everyone is devastated, but that word doesn't feel like enough to explain how we are all feeling," she wrote. "Our sweet, funny boy with a cheeky smile that could make anyone's heart full. The smile that we can't see again. "I didn't even get to tell you how much I love you."
