Entertainment REPORT: Jennifer Lopez Turned Away From High End Store (Photo by David Becker/Getty Images) Leena Nasir Entertainment Reporter August 08, 2025 11:58 AM ET August 08, 2025 11:58 AM ET Leena Nasir Entertainment Reporter Font Size: A security guard in a Chanel store in Turkey reportedly refused to allow Jennifer Lopez into the store during her August 4th shopping spree in the region. The famous singer was in Instanbul to perform at Yenikapi Festival Park as part of her “Up All Night Tour” and took some time to shop at the region’s high-end fashion stores the day before the show, Türkiye Today reported. She encountered a surprising moment when the security guard inside the Chanel store refused to allow her entry. In a surprisingly non-diva moment, Lopez reportedly didn’t offer much of a reaction to being denied. “Okay, no problem,” she said before turning and walking away, the outlet reported.
