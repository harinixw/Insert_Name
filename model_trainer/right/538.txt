Police Reportedly Arrest Jack Nicholson’s Grandson For Domestic Violence
Entertainment Police Reportedly Arrest Jack Nicholson’s Grandson For Domestic Violence (Photo by Stephen Dunn/Getty Images) Leena Nasir Entertainment Reporter August 08, 2025 12:20 PM ET August 08, 2025 12:20 PM ET Leena Nasir Entertainment Reporter Font Size: Los Angeles police reportedly arrested the Jack Nicholson’s eldest grandson Aug. 5 on a felony domestic violence charge. Police accused Sean Norfleet, the son of Jack Nicholson’s daughter Jennifer and her ex-husband Mark Norfleet, of attacking an unidentified woman, according arrest records obtained by People. Details surrounding the nature of the alleged attack and the identity of the woman were not made public. Authorities released Norfleet on $50,000 bond the very same day he was arrested and taken into police custody.
