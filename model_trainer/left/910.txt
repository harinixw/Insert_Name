Poem Faith A poem By Kevin Young Lucy Murray Willis August 3, 2025 Share Save Explore the September 2025 Issue Check out more from this issue and find your next story to read. View More How do the small birds in the street know how not to die— that whatever they gather, hunger for, is never enough to keep them in the road when our wheels bear down upon them? They feast on what I cannot see then fly away & sing. This poem appears in the September 2025 print edition. About the Author Kevin Young Kevin Young is the author of 16 books of poetry and prose, including, most recently, Night Watch.
