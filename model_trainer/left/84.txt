By David Moye Senior Reporter, HuffPost Aug 7, 2025, 01:41 PM EDT LEAVE A COMMENT LOADINGERROR LOADING Vice President JD Vance tried to put on a brave face in reaction to a humiliating portrayal on Wednesday’s “South Park” episode, but the internet wouldn’t let him get away with it. The episode portrayed Donald Trump’s Mar-a-Lago home as a sort of “Fantasy Island” with the president as Mr. Roarke and Vance as his assistant Tattoo.
